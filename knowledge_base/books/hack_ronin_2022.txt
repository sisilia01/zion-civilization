# Ronin Bridge Hack 2022
# Amount lost: $625 million
# Vulnerability: Social engineering + private key compromise

Ronin is the blockchain for Axie Infinity.
The bridge used 9 validators — 5 needed to approve withdrawals.
Attacker compromised 5 validator private keys through:
1. Fake job offer PDF sent to senior engineer (spear phishing)
2. PDF contained malware that extracted private keys
3. With 5 keys — attacker controlled the bridge
Lesson: Human element is the weakest link (Mitnick was right).
No amount of smart contract security matters if private keys are compromised.
Constitutional parallel: ZION ZCO Tribunal needs key security.
