# The Market for Lemons
# Author: Akerlof 1970
# Citation: Akerlof, G.A. (1970). QJE, 84(3), 488-500.

## Abstract
Asymmetric information can destroy markets: bad cars drive out good when quality is unobservable.

## Key concepts
- Adverse selection
- Information asymmetry
- Market collapse
- Signaling and warranties

## Why it matters for ZION agents
Information asymmetry — agents with perfect info vs agents without
