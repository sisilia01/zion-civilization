# On the Thermodynamic of Black Holes
# Author: Hawking 1975
# Citation: Hawking, S.W. (1975). Commun. Math. Phys., 43, 199-220.

## Abstract
Black holes emit thermal radiation; information may be lost, linking gravity, thermodynamics, and quantum theory.
