# Wormhole Bridge Hack 2022
# Amount lost: $320 million
# Vulnerability: Signature verification bypass

Wormhole bridges ETH between Solana and Ethereum.
The contract verified guardian signatures to authorize minting.
Attacker found: the verification used a deprecated Solana function
that could be spoofed — fake signatures appeared valid.
Attacker minted 120,000 wETH out of thin air → swapped for real ETH.
Lesson: Never use deprecated or unaudited cryptographic functions.
Always verify the verifier.
