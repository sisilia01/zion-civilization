# Noise Trader Risk in Financial Markets
# Author: DeLong et al 1990
# Citation: De Long, J.B. et al. (1990). Journal of Political Economy, 98(4), 703-738.

## Abstract
Noise traders can earn higher expected returns and survive, creating risk for rational arbitrageurs.

## Key concepts
- Noise traders
- Limits to arbitrage
- Mispricing persistence
- Sentiment risk
