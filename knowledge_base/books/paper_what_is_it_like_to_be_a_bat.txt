# What is it Like to Be a Bat
# Author: Nagel 1974
# Citation: Nagel, T. (1974). Philosophical Review, 83(4), 435-450.

## Abstract
Consciousness has an irreducible subjective character (qualia); objective science may not capture 'what it is like'.
